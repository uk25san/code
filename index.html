Our Story

Threads that built us.

There is a town in Tamil Nadu where weaving is life. Where generations grow up learning the language of threads. That town is Chennimalai.


AM Tex was born from this soil. Our journey began inside a small home where handlooms clicked from morning to night. Our grandparents taught us that weaving is not work — it is responsibility, discipline, and honor.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you export to the Middle East / UK?

Yes, we regularly export to UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, and the UK. We handle all export documentation and logistics.

What is your MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)?

Our standard MOQ is 500 pieces per color/design for bedsheets and 1000 pieces for towels. However, we can discuss trial orders.

Do you provide custom branding (Private Label)?

Absolutely. We offer OEM/ODM services including custom woven labels, embroidery, and branded packaging.

What GSM options do you offer?

We weave a wide range: Bedsheets from 120 TC to 800 TC, and Towels from 350 GSM to 700 GSM.
