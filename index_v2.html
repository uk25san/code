Our Story

Threads that built us.

There is a town in Tamil Nadu where weaving is life. Where generations grow up learning the language of threads. That town is Chennimalai.


AM Tex was born from this soil. Our journey began inside a small home where handlooms clicked from morning to night. Our grandparents taught us that weaving is not work — it is responsibility, discipline, and honor.

“If it’s from Chennimalai, you can sell it confidently.”

— A common saying among textile buyers in India.
